# Project Descriptions

## End-to-end event styling

**Event:** Intimate Birthday Celebration
**Theme:** "Indoor Camping & Movie Night"

Designed the *end-to-end flow* to make an at-home birthday feel extraordinary. Starting with a *cozy indoor camping tent setup with ambient lighting* to enjoy with kids. An *at-home fine-dine tablescape* setup for the couple, followed by a *romantic movie night experience on the projector* paired with wine and *personalized charcuterie boards*.

*Layered the space* using props, textures, and *personalized details* to create the perfect ambience for an *intimate but memorable evening.*

## Balloon arch and backdrop styling

### Willow Glen's Annual Holi Celebration

**Event:** Willow Glen's Annual Holi Celebration
**Theme:** "Holi Hai"

Designed a *vibrant yet performance-friendly visual anchor* for the event, celebrating the joy, color, and cultural spirit of Holi. Covered the frame with richly colored fabric drapes arranged in a *vertical color story* creating a clean, seamless canvas. Added *depth and festive character* through *handcrafted playful accents* and balloons with wrapped marigold garlands to visually frame the performance area.
